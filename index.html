About TextVault

TextVault is a fast, secure, entirely client-side text scrambler designed to protect sensitive messages.

How it works: By running recursive Base64 encoding completely in your browser, it adds a 3x multiplier security layer of obfuscation to your text. Because everything happens locally, your data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.

Auto-Clipboard: For maximum convenience, the app automatically copies the encrypted or decrypted result directly to your clipboard the moment you press a button. You don't even need to view the output to share it!
